Brain Assymetries in the Superior Temporal Sulcus

At Brigham Young University, I worked for about two years as a research assistant for Dr. Jared Nielsen trying to find whether assymetries in the brain have any influence on language development in children.

Worst Case Scenario

In my design studies, I was part of a team that created an app interface for emergency preparedness and critical emergency response. We built it to give response instruction for anyone wanting to prepare for emergencies as well as rapid and dependable instruction for critical emergencies when first responders have not yet arrived.

Emergency Preparedness 101

Shortly after I worked on Worst Case Scenario, I began working with a non-profit called the Disaster Discovery Center. Our team was asked to design a travelling exhibit the DDC could take to schools and universities to teach disaster response and preparedness. I was part of a smaller team tasked with designing a web interface with which students would interact to proceed through the physical environment. This interface would prompt the students with disaster situations that would test their personal preparedness and their knowledge of survival scenarios.
